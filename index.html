    Project Idea

    A Pet Keeping Information App

    What Is the Idea?

    I wish to create an app that provides an exhaustive collection of information on a wide variety of pets. I hope this app provides valuable information for new or old pet owners in order to increase the general wellbeing of their beloved friends.

    Why Is It Important/Interesting?

    A 2019 study done by Animal Medicines Australiashows that “61% of Australian households have a pet”. Most of those pets are dogs (40%) or cats (27%), with over 60% of owners describing their pet as a member of the family. Australia also has higher rates of pet ownership than both the United States and the United Kingdom.

    These high pet ownership rates tell me that Australians really love having animal companions around. I would like my app to provide support to the many people who already own pets and anyone who is looking to get a pet in the future.

    This app is important because there are many myths, missing information, misinformation and dangerous information in pet keeping. I wish for my app to provide safe information for the huge population of Australian pet owners. I also hope to directly link pet owners to all of the basic and complex information about their individual pets. I imagine this app could aid people and pets by:

    • Reducing the frequency of preventable illnesses.
    • Informing people of a pet’s care requirements before or after they welcome a pet into their home.
    • Providing training guides to reduce behavioural problems or increase the bond between owners and pets.
    • Linking pet owners to invaluable products and resources.
    • Encouraging responsible pet ownership or reducing cases of neglect.
    • Providing easy/quick access for the user, saving time often spent sifting through the internet to find trustworthy information.
    • Giving answers to the most FAQs that owners have about many kinds of pets.

    What Are the Drawbacks of This App or The Potential Obstacles I May Face?

    This app would require a lot of input from experts such as vets or pet trainers. This would require much research, collaboration, time and potentially money to pay for their input. Unfortunately, while pet care is a passion/hobby of mine, I am neither qualified nor an expert in this area. Therefore, after developing my skills in IT I could create, run and manage the app but I will not be able to create the core content it displays. At this stage I do not have any connections to vets, pet trainers or animal experts of any kind. I would need to make these connections in order to make the app fully functional. This may be quite interesting for me so not necessarily a drawback.

    Alternatively, or in some cases, instead of uploading original information to the app I could simply direct users to external - but still trustworthy - resources. This might involve writing a description of what the resource is, how the resource is helpful and then linking to the external website. Though I foresee that being an issue if I wish to make money from this app. Encouraging the consumer to leave or close the app is not ideal. I am not sure how but I think it is possible to display someone else’s content - such as a dog training YouTube video - on my app while still crediting the creator and ensuring that the creator’s potential profit on the video is not hindered. I believe I would need to “imbed” the YouTube video into a part of my app, that way the user can watch it and seamlessly return to learning about pet care within the app.

    If I do wish to create some of my own content (e.g. in-app mini games or fun facts) I could reference articles, research, etc. That way I would not need to employ/ask other people to supply the content.

    On top of that, I have never made an app before and have little/no experience in the IT sector. I have a lot to learn before I will see this app come to life.

    What Features Will the App Have?

    The app will include a long list of features that I hope will be useful to all pet users. I plan to start with the first 1-3 features on this list and add the rest of the resources later as I learn more about what is plausible.

    1. Pet Care Guides

      2. I intend for the Pet Care Guides to inform users on the basic or complex needs of a variety of pets. The aim for this feature is to help users choose suitable animals to join their families and prevent unexpected complications. Many thousands of pets end up in shelters every year, many of which are surrendered due to pet keepers not anticipating the care requirements of their pets before purchasing them. My app will provide an easy way for people to learn about their pets before purchasing or adopting their new friend.

    2. FAQ/Frequently Asked Questions

      3. I would like to include a Frequently Asked Questions section so that users can find answers promptly. Often pet keepers wonder things like “can my dog eat this?” or “is this toy safe for my dog to play with?” and the answers that are given at pet stores are not always accurate. For example, rawhide dog treats and some rope toys are not safe for dogs to play with or consume, despite them often being sold at pet stores. If dog keepers had access to a FAQ hub of accurate information then they could find answers to these very important questions with ease. Initially I would begin by basing the questions off of Google’s most frequently asked pet questions but I would eventually like to cater the questions to what the users of the app are most interested in knowing about.

    3. Fun Facts

      4. Let’s face it, animals are weird and wonderful beings. The way that they adapt to their environments amazes humans and makes us curious to know more. The Fun Facts feature aims to encourage this curiosity and make our pet keeping research fun and engaging. This feature would include facts about both domesticated and wild animals. I will probably make this a weekly or daily feature, rather than just a constant list of facts. I hope that will encourage ongoing learning and education within the app. This may be a pop-up feature within the app that doesn’t take up much of the reader’s time. Alternatively, it could appear on the loading screen or as a daily/weekly notification in the mornings.

    4. Pet Profile

      5. This is where the user can fill in some basic information about their pet so that the app can suggest related information to the user. This may include fill in sections such as the pets name and drop-down boxes for species, breed and age.

    5. Breed Information

      6. Within the pet keeping hobby there are many breeds and crossbreeds that people recognise and love. There are also many breeds that people have never heard of that they may wish to know more about. This section will mostly cater to the owners of dogs due to their long list of breeds and vastly different features. Temperament, appearance, energy level, common illnesses, grooming requirements and size vary greatly depending on the dog breed. This information is valuable to me and to others who are also passionate about their pets.

    6. Latest Research/Articles

      7. The “Latest Research” or “Articles” section will share the most recent information being talked about in the news, magazines or online. This section is for the animal lovers who want who are passionate about all fauna found on earth. This may include animal research, trending footage, documentary recommendations, discoveries, endangered species, the impact of climate change on animals and much more. I will need to be able to imbed these articles into my app or provide links to external websites.

    7. Quick Tips

      8. The Quick Tips section will function similarly to the Fun Facts section in that it may only be a daily or weekly feature via a pop-up, loading screen or notification. These could be catered to the user by using their basic pet info to supply relevant tips. E.g. Dog owners would receive tips on dog care and turtle owners would receive tips on turtle care.

    8. Training Guides

      9. The Training Guide section would include embedded or links to YouTube videos on some of the best training advice available. This will hopefully encourage pet exercise, promote bonding and reduce difficult or dangerous behaviours.

    9. Essential Supplies

      10. The Essential Supplies section will direct users towards the most important items, the must have products that they for their pet’s care. This may be an opportunity to include affiliate links and provide a source of income rather than using advertisements. I wish for this section include top quality items, to just products that will make a quick buck. This would likely involve linking to external websites seeing as I would not be selling the products within my app.

    10. Merchandise

      11. I may one day include a Merchandise section that includes pet care items of my own creation. This may be another form of income for the app in the future. This would involve creating an in-app store or perhaps creating my own external website and linking to it. Either way this would involve a lot of research into privacy, security, online transactions and how to set that up within the app/website.

    11. Connect to A Vet

      12. An in-app chat feature that connects users directly to vets to receive general medical advice. I foresee some issues with this as vet care almost always requires examining the patient to accurately identify or rule out common issues. But general questions or behavioural advicemay be answered by using this feature.
